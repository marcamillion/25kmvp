Project Overview

A database of high school soccer players, built for College & University Soccer Coaches and Recruits. This platform was designed to make the recruting process streamlined with all necessary info of each player easily available (including videos of their gameplay, high school transcripts and much more).

Product Development Strategy & Execution

Recrutz was a database for both Recruits and Players, essentially a dual-sided marketplace. We faced the typical chicken & egg problem. We decided to manually find both coaches & players. Both required different strategies and tactics, but both were successfully pursued.

For coaches, we developed a B2B, Cold Email Sales Strategy where we prospected potential leads based on our customer development results and built a sales pipeline infrastructure for reaching out and converting these leads.

For recruits, we established partnerships with the top 7 high schools (at the principal level). From there we developed relationships with the coaches and worked with them to choose the best candidates for the first cohort. From there we engaged the players & their families and staged a showcase match where their gameplay was recorded and used as the primary video for the platform.
