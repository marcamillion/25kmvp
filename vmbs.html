VMBS - Victoria Mutual Building Society

 Meat Box| CompVersions  

Project Overview

VMBS is a financial institution that has been around since 1878. Their bread & butter services are typically related to mortgages and financial services around home building in general. The primary goal of this project was to increase awareness of a newly designed & launched savings product called 'iSave'.

Strategy & Execution

Our main approach to this project was to develop and design high-level strategic direction for the campaign to help consumers understand the benefits of saving in general, and the benefits of this particular savings product. The early results have been quite promising.

Not only did we conceive and develop the creative direction of the campaign, but to kick it off we executed the shooting of both the primary TV ad spot (top video on the right), and additional complentary ad spots to support the primary video asset. The video has been leveraged in both traditional media and social media (Instagram, Facebook & YouTube).

The 'coach' character that we developed in the primary video asset, has become a psuedo spokesperson for the brand in a bigger way than was initially expected. So we doubled down on that and have been leveraging that success to generate spin-off content with that coach. We also provide email marketing support.
