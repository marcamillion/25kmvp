Meat Box - Fresh Meat Cuts Delivered Right To Your Door

 10 Pound Pledge| VMBS  

Project Overview

A service that delivers freshly cut meat directly to your front door. No traffic, no lines, no stress. The primary goal of this project was to drive revenue growth in the most cost-effective way possible.

Strategy & Execution

Meat Box was born out of an existing, family-run catering business that has been around for more than 2 decades. The youngest son involved in the business realized that they could leverage their existing infrastructure and supplier relationships to build a home delivery service for fresh cuts of meat. So we helped them develop branding, social media marketing strategies, email marketing strategies & content creation (graphic design, copywriting, etc.).

We gave them a turn-key service that included everything from building the initial financial dashboard framework so they could fully understand unit economics, along with accounting & invoicing tools and helping to design business processes to support the expansion.

Our general approach to this project was to experiment with many different channels, and double down on those that worked. We experimented with offline channels (mailbox mailers, radio & press interviews, for example) and a variety of online channels (email marketing, Instagram & Facebook).

Mid-stream into the project, we came upon conditions that required a potential rebranding. We explored options and presented them to the client as if we needed to make the decision ourselves for our business. Ultimately, we executed a complete rebranding of all assets and developed key talking points to explain the rebranding.
