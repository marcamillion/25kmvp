10 Pound Pledge - In-Home Workout & Nutrition Guide

 Recrutz| Meat Box  

Project Overview

We created a workout & nutrition program that people could do in the comfort of their own home, on their TV, computer, iPad or mobile device.

Product Development, Strategy & Execution

We started this project from scratch. We worked on the fitness & nutrition content, then turned those into a series of videos & a nutrition ebook. We developed packaging & pricing strategies, implemented the complete user experience from initial customer contact to post-purchase support.

We got a custom soundtrack made for the program, and to keep the authentically Jamaican-feel to the project, we negotiated licensing arrangements with some successful reggae/dancehall music producers.

The workouts in the videos was designed to be as accessible as possible, including weight alternatives that could be used in the High-Intensity Interval Training program.

On top of actually building the product, we also built the branding, website, implemented full e-commerce capabilities and implemented customer support infrastructure to support post-purchase inquiries.
